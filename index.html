OIC DEVAGIRI

Office of International Collaboration (OIC) Devagiri aims to enhance our global partnerships through academic and cultural exchange. We are working together to advance the discovery of new knowledge, innovational technology development, strategic education, and professional training. Our mission is to strengthen the communities being served through a positive and productive approach. Devagiri has already entered into agreements with prestigious universities and institutes from around the world. Such professional collaborations with includes: the Intelligence Research Institute (IRI) San Diego (USA), Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU), UniversitiTeknologi Mara, Selangor Malaysia, ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants), CMA (Certified Management Accountant) US & IMA (Institute of Management Accountant). OIC’s mission the students, faculty, and professions alike in seeking to tackle the emerging challenges and future requirements with proactive approaches.

About Us

St. Josephs College Devagiri (Autonomous)

Envisioned by the CMI Congregation and established in 1956, St. Josephs College Devagiri (Autonomous), affiliated to the University of Calicut has been imparting quality education for the last 66 years as one of the most prestigious Arts & Science Colleges in Malabar. True to its Motto, PRO DEO ET PATRIA (For God and Country), the institution works with commitment to create a just and humane society, upholding national integration and religious harmony, with special emphasis on the enrichment of the poor and the marginalized

The Intelligence Research Institute (IRI)

The Intelligence Research Institute (IRI) is continuously monitoring events, aggregating information, conducting analysis, and formalizing content into briefings to fulfill its vision of providing intelligence as a service. IRI delivers strategic seminars, workshops, and capacity building programs through academic and commercial partners globally. The mission is to serve as a global leader in training and education to detect, deter, and defend against emerging threats in a timeline "Left of Boom".
